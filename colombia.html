Welcome to Colombia

Location

Colombia is located on the northern region of South America. Due to its varying terrains, Colombia has different natural regions, each with their own characteristics. These regions can be classified into six groups: 1. Andes mountain 2. Plains 3. Carribean coast 4. Amazon Rainforest 5. Islands in Pacific and Atlantic 6. Pacific Coastal Range

Politics

Juan Manuel Santos Calderón is the current President of Colombia. He assumed his role as the 32nd president on the 7th of August, 2010.
