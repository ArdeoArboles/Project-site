Welcome to Brazil

Location

Feast your eyes on the one, the only, Brazil.

With more than three million square miles, Brazil is the largest country in South America. Due to its size, Brazil occupies the majority of South America's eastern coast as well as a large portion of the continent's interior. It is so large that it spans four time zones and borders every country in South America except for Ecuador and Chile. When juxtaposed to other countres, Brazil is the fifth largest country in the world.
